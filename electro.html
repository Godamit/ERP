WHAT IS ELECTROVERSE TSEC ?

Electroverse.Comm-TSEC, A Technical Committee of the EXTC Department at Thadomal Shahani Engineering College, Mumbai, Maharashtra, successfully held its inaugural event in 2003, marking a significant milestone in fostering innovation and collaboration. The committee emphasizes interdisciplinary cooperation, exemplified by events like the Non-Stop Hardware Hackathon, where students from diverse departments converge to share ideas and skills, enriching their problem-solving approaches.
